Current Fire Restrictions

The current park-wide fire hazzard is medium. Campers may build small campfires in designated fire areas only. All fires must be inside fire rings or fire pits.

This time of year is perfect for sitting around the campfire. With moderate to cold weather and ample precipitation, wildfires are generally not a high risk in winter months.
